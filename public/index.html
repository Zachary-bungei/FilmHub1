Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit my film idea?

You can submit your film idea using our submission form. Make sure to provide a clear concept, storyline, and target audience.

2. Is there a fee to submit my idea?

Yes, there is a small processing fee to cover administrative costs and ensure serious submissions.

3. Can I submit multiple ideas?

Absolutely! Each idea should be submitted separately with its own form and payment.

4. How do I know my idea is safe?

All submissions are encrypted and stored securely. You retain ownership of your ideas until an agreement is made.

5. Can I sell my idea directly to producers?

Yes, our platform connects you with vetted producers. We provide guidelines to ensure secure transactions.

6. How long does it take to get feedback?

Typically, feedback is provided within 2-3 weeks, depending on the complexity of your idea and producer availability.

7. Do I need a registered copyright?

While not required, we recommend registering your idea with your local copyright office for added protection.

8. Can I edit my submission after sending?

Yes, you can update your submission within 48 hours of sending it. After that, edits may require contacting support.

9. How are payments handled?

Payments are securely processed via our platform. We release funds only after agreements between you and the buyer are finalized.

10. Who can I contact for support?

You can reach our support team via email or chat on the platform. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
